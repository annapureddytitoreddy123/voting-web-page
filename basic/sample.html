Services - Election Commission

Welcome to the services provided by the Election Commission. Here are the available services:

  • Voter Registration: Register to vote for upcoming elections.
  • Election Information: Get information about upcoming elections, candidates, and polling locations.
  • Grievance Filing: File a grievance related to election processes or issues.
  • Result Declaration: Access election results after the voting process.

If you have any queries or need assistance, feel free to contact us.
